Entertainment

Seaplane Ride

A seaplane excursion in Maldives is more than a treat you could ask for as you get a total birds eye view of the islands, sandbars, and lagoons. Tourists can watch the main attractions of Maldives such as Hulhumale Island and Male Island from a different point of view.

Jet Ski

Jet skiing is a high-speed water entertainment that is popular around the world as both a recreational activity and a competitive kind of sport. allow you to discover hidden bays and beautiful beaches. It gives more enjoyment while riding on jet skis.

Scuba Dive

Maaya Thila is home to tons of marine life and is an excellent site for Scuba Diving in the Maldives. It is about 80 meters in diameter and houses caves & overhangs. Interestingly, Maaya Thila is suitable for Night Diving as well. It makes you feel good and makes you refresh.
